Networking Fundamentals

What is Computer Networking?

Computer networking is the process of connecting computers, devices and systems to share data and resources.

Important Networking Topics

  • OSI Model
  • TCP/IP Model
  • IP Addressing
  • Subnetting
  • VLAN
  • Routing
  • DNS
  • DHCP
  • TCP and UDP

Why Learn Networking?

Networking is the foundation of cybersecurity. Understanding networks helps security professionals identify vulnerabilities, analyze traffic and protect systems.
