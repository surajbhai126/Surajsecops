What is Cybersecurity?

Published: July 2026

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, applications, devices and data from unauthorized access, attacks and damage.

Why is Cybersecurity Important?

In today's digital world, organizations store large amounts of sensitive data online. Cybersecurity helps protect this data from cyber attacks and unauthorized access.

Types of Cybersecurity

  • Network Security
  • Application Security
  • Cloud Security
  • Information Security
  • Endpoint Security

Conclusion

Cybersecurity is an important field that protects digital systems, networks and information from cyber threats.
