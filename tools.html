Base64 Encoder / Decoder

Encode text into Base64 or decode Base64 data directly in your browser.

What is Base64?

Base64 is an encoding method that converts binary data into text characters. It is commonly used in web applications, email systems and data transmission.

⚠️ Base64 is encoding, not encryption. It should not be used to protect sensitive data.
